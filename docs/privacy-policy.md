# Privacy Policy — C64 Commander

**Effective date:** 19 January 2026

## Overview

C64 Commander does not collect, store, or share personal data. The app operates locally on your device and communicates only with your C64 Ultimate device on your network.

## Data collection

- **Personal data:** Not collected.
- **Analytics:** Not used.
- **Crash reporting:** Not used.
- **Advertising:** Not used.
- **Login/Account:** Not required.

## Network communication

The app sends requests to the C64 Ultimate device you configure (for example, over your local network). These requests are necessary to read and update device settings and to send SID files for playback.

## Third-party software

The app includes open-source libraries to provide UI and device integration. A full list is available in [THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md](../THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md).

## Children’s privacy

The app does not target children and does not collect any personal information.

## Changes

If this policy changes, we will update this page.
